## QBO Reports for KPIs

### Report Schedule

| KPI Category | QBO Report | Frequency |
|--------------|------------|-----------|
| Profitability | P&L by Customer | Monthly |
| Cash Position | Statement of Cash Flows | Weekly |
| AR/DSO | A/R Aging Summary | Weekly |
| AP/DPO | A/P Aging Summary | Weekly |
| Labor | Payroll Summary | Bi-weekly |
| Job Costs | Job Cost Detail | Monthly |

### Custom Report Setup

1. **Gross Profit by Project:**
   - Reports → Profit & Loss by Customer
   - Customize → Filter by Date Range

2. **Cash Flow Trend:**
   - Reports → Statement of Cash Flows
   - Run monthly, track in dashboard

3. **DSO Calculation:**
   - Reports → A/R Aging Summary
   - Compare to Revenue from P&L
