**Lien Waiver Types:**
```
CONDITIONAL WAIVER ON PROGRESS PAYMENT
- Provided with pay application
- Becomes effective when payment clears
- For progress payments during project

UNCONDITIONAL WAIVER ON PROGRESS PAYMENT
- Provided after payment received
- Confirms prior payment was received
- No conditions attached

CONDITIONAL WAIVER ON FINAL PAYMENT
- Provided with final pay application
- Covers all work and materials
- Effective when final payment clears

UNCONDITIONAL WAIVER ON FINAL PAYMENT
- Provided after final payment received
- Releases all lien rights
- Required before retention release
```

## Tips for Maintaining Compliance

1. **Calendar Expiration Dates** - Set reminders 60 days before expirations
2. **Don't Pay Without Documents** - Establish policy requiring compliance
3. **Use Checklists** - Consistent process prevents gaps
4. **Verify, Don't Trust** - Check licenses and insurance directly
5. **Document Everything** - Keep organized files by subcontractor
6. **Review Regularly** - Monthly compliance review catches issues early
7. **Train Your Team** - Everyone should know the requirements
8. **Use Technology** - Consider compliance tracking software for large operations
