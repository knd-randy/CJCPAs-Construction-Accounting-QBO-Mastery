# Daily & Weekly Operational Checklists

## Overview

Consistent daily and weekly routines are the foundation of accurate construction accounting. These checklists ensure nothing falls through the cracks and keep your books in real-time condition.

This guide provides detailed checklists for:
- **Daily tasks** (15-30 minutes)
- **Weekly tasks** (1-2 hours)
- **By role** (Bookkeeper, Office Manager, Controller)

### QBO Daily Routine - Step by Step

#### Step 1: Bank Feeds (5-10 minutes)

**HOW:**

1. Log into QBO
2. Go to **Banking** (or **Transactions** → **Banking**)
3. Check each connected account:
   - Look for green "For Review" count
   - Work through each transaction

**For Each Transaction:**

| Action | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Match** | Transaction already recorded (payment, transfer) |
| **Add** | New transaction needing categorization |
| **Transfer** | Money moving between accounts |
| **Exclude** | Duplicate or personal transaction |

**Construction-Specific:**
- Always assign **Class/Project** to job costs
- Use consistent vendor names
- Split transactions hitting multiple jobs

#### Step 2: Enter Bills (5-10 minutes)

**HOW:**

1. Go to **+ New** → **Bill**
2. Select **Vendor**
3. Enter **Bill Date** and **Due Date**
4. Enter line items with:
   - **Category** (expense or COGS account)
   - **Description**
   - **Amount**
   - **Customer/Project** (for job costs)
   - **Class** (if using classes)
5. Attach the invoice image
6. Save

**Tips:**
- Enter bills the day received (don't batch)
- Verify PO match if applicable
- Double-check job assignment

#### Step 3: Record Payments Received (5 minutes)

**HOW:**

1. Go to **+ New** → **Receive Payment**
2. Select **Customer**
3. Enter **Payment Date** and **Amount**
4. Select invoices being paid
5. Choose **Deposit to** account (Undeposited Funds or bank)
6. Save

**For Partial Payments:**
- Apply to specific invoices
- Note short-pay reason in memo
- Follow up on balance

#### Step 4: Make Deposits (2-3 minutes)

**HOW:**

1. Go to **+ New** → **Bank Deposit**
2. Select payments to include in deposit
3. Verify total matches bank deposit slip
4. Select account and date
5. Save

## Tips for Checklist Success

### 1. Make It a Habit

- Same time every day/week
- Don't skip "because it's slow"
- Build into your routine

### 2. Customize for Your Business

- Remove tasks that don't apply
- Add items specific to your operations
- Adjust timing to match your workflow

### 3. Use Technology

- Create recurring tasks in your calendar
- Use checklist apps (Notion, Asana, etc.)
- Set up QBO reminders

### 4. Track Completion

- Keep completed checklists for reference
- Review patterns (what gets skipped?)
- Adjust as needed

### 5. Accountability

- Assign specific people to each checklist
- Review completion in weekly meetings
- Address chronic incomplete items
